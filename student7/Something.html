PINGPONG TEST

Can you make a small ping pong front end app with HTML5? Basically it has 2 square wall and can kick the ball the each other, don't think it to be very complicated, you should be able to finish this within 3 hours. Just want to see a ball can be kicked from left to right and from right to left, and the ball can be a square as well.

Work based on the tutorial of Cristian Nistor published at lostresort.BIZ.

Use the W, S and UP, DOWN keys.
