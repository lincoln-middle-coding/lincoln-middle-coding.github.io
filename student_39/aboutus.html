About Legend Box Builds

How did we start

We started in October when someone asked a few of us to make a full set of armor. After 25 hours over the course of 16 days we had completed our project.
But after we had finished, we decided that we were going to sell it instead of giving it out for free.
After selling the set of armor, we realized our potential for making objects out of cardboard. and decided to create a small business. People started coming to us and asking us for cardboard toys, weapons, and clothing.
We started this website a month after LegendBoxBuilds originated and want to share our story.

Why us?

Our business creates whatever you want, however you want. We use packaging to create what you would like.
